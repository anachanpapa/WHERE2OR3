How to open the Prayer Group

  1. click the "Open the Prayer Group" button.
  2. Sign in to Google. (if you have not signed in to Google yet)
  3. Install the Hangouts plugin. (only the first time)
  4. In the invitation popup click the "Close" button.
  5. Start praying for brothers and sisters and wait for other prayer partners to join in.

* When you finish praying, just close the Hangouts page.

How to join in the Prayer Group

  1. click the "Join in the Prayer Group" button
  2. Sign in to Google. (if you have not signed in to Google yet)
  3. Install the Hangouts plugin. (only the first time)
  4. Click the "Join" button.
  5. Start praying for brothers and sisters with other prayer partners.

* When you finish praying, just close the Hangouts page.
